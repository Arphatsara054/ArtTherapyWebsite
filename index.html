About Us

Our Aims

It is a confusing world we are living in, especially adjusting to the new norm with the pandemic. Flow aims to bring a fun way to cope and manage your emotions and stress in a difficult time like this.

Our Values

Creativity

We aim to give you the tools to embrace your inner artist.

Fun

We like to keep the activities easy, enjoyable and tailored to your busy schedule.

Inner Harmony

To bring calm to your mind and find coping strategies that works for you.

Curiosity

Our aim is to provide you tools to explore your emotions, what triggers it and what styles works for you.

At flow, we aim to to make art accessible and use it to explore oneself. Hence the exitence of this website. This website has everything that you could need in order to get started with your art therapy. On the art slide you can fill out a questionnare to have a few hand-selected images to insipre your work, because we know how difficult it can be to get the ball rolling. Alongside this on the music slide we encourage you to select some mood music to put you into the headspace of actually drawing and allow you to set a timer (if you would like!) so you can incorporate art therapy into your everday life. And finally, what do you use to draw? Check out our starter set below to support us and get started on your art! And of course, if you have any questions please do not hesitate to contact us via the contact tab.

In order to get the full experience of this website and what it has to offer - to make it the most accessible. We want you to know which form of art you want to create, and what works for you. The art is about you, not about the objective quality of art. So we want to give you the best opportunity to try something new and all types of art, so we have put together a set. Including pencil, charcoal and pens.
When you recieve your set, you will be given a month to use it to your own capacity along with the elements of the website as laid out above. In order to reduce waste, if there are any tools which you have not taken a liking to and will not be using - please return them in the prepaid package attached to the set. Upon recieving the returned components you will be refunded part of the original price.
