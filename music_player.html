Your Music

This page is here for you to have everything at your fingertips to start on your art therapy and fully be immersed in it. We have curated some tracks indicative of different moods but we do not want to sway how you think of them so have not labelled them as such. Rather take your time listening to them and use the length of the tracks to decide how long your session will be for today. As much as we have tried to cover everything here it may just not be what you are looking for today - and that's okay! Go to FMA to look through more tracks. But, remember the most important part is to start drawing so just try to let go. You got this!
