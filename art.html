Your Art

In this page, we provide some art techniques for you to try out and find what works for you. You will receive a pack of art activities flashcards included when ordering your art supplies.


Postcard Activity

A good self-discovery exercise that helps answer the question, “what would I say to someone if I didn’t have to do it face-to-face?”




Lighthouse Activity

For these who feel lost, overwhelmed, or isolated, expressing those feeling and visualise hope can be therapeutic and a great way to identify needs, feel hope for the future.
